Research Areas

  • My research focuses on the application of Machine Learning in seismology, particularly in event clustering and uncovering new seismological discoveries.
  • I am also interested in the evolution and physical mechanisms of foreshocks, aftershocks and mainshocks.
  • Additionally, I have worked on the earthquake cycles, specifically the simulation of coseismic and postseismic deformation.
